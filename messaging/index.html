3. Narrative Description

Users enter their phone number and check the consent box to join our SMS updates. When the form is submitted, we store:

  • Phone number
  • Timestamp
  • IP address

We use this as proof of consent for A2P messaging. Users can opt out anytime by replying STOP, or by using our opt-out form:
